cryptmount

- mount from script
    cryptmount --passwd-fd 5 jsun_test 5< <(echo eroas)

- empty password is allowed
    cryptmount --passwd-fd 5 jsun_test 5< <(echo )

- another way
    exec 10<<<"eroas"
    cryptmount --passwd-fd 10 jsun_test
